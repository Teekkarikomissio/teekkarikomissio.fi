# Associations Meetings

## Autumn Meeting 2025

Teekkari Commission’s autumn meeting that will take place on **Monday, December 15th at 17.00 in Argentum, Aurum** (Henrikinkatu 2, 20500 Turku). 

At the meeting, the board and functionaries for the year 2026 will be elected. The board positions include Chair of the Board, Vice Chair of the Board, and other board members (tot 8: Secretary, Treasurer, External Relations Commissary, Teekkari Culture Commissary, Communications Commissary, Liaison Commissary, and 2 Events Commissaries), who will divide the specific roles among themselves later.

The functionary positions are Workspace Manager, Webmaster, 1–2 Graphic Designers, and the Operations Auditors (2 regular, 2 deputies). Applying for the Dipping Committee will be held separately.

The meeting is open to everyone. Anyone of the member associations members may run for a position. If you cannot attend in person but wish to run for a position, please contact beforehand.

The budget and the plan of activities för 2026 will also be on the agenda as well as eventual uppdates in regulations. The agenda will be found only in Finnish and Swedish.
